Who We Are

SY GREEN ENERGY. established in the year of 2022, is the leading service provider and consultant for an exclusive range of domestic and commercial Bio-gas plants, dairy farm solutions, Oxygen generation plant, Nitrogen generation plant, solar plants, waste to energy solutions, and organic fertilizers. Our company was founded on the principle of delivering high quality and "Nature Friendly" solutions to our clients which in terns benefits our clients and mother nature.
Our company has sufficient skilled and engineering professionals who provide infrastructural facilities to undertake all domestic, industrial, and commercial projects related to renewable energy. Our company renders consultancy services for the preparation of projects, takes up feasibility studies, and site visits and carries our project implementation and awareness program on waste management. Our company provides a one-step solution for all waste-to-energy projects.
Our people are highly qualified, experienced, and creative professionals. We believe that the results we achieve are driven by the insight of our staff, coupled with our robust methods. We are adept at analyzing the client's needs, developing targeted, high-impact organizational interventions, and deploying solutions to close the strategy-execution gap, producing measurable bottom line results.
